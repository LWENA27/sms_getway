## ğŸ“ First Time Users

### Sign Up
1. Enter your email address
2. Create a strong password
3. Click "Sign Up"

### Create Workspace
1. Choose workspace name (e.g., "My Organization")
2. Click "Create"
3. Done! You're ready to send SMS

### Grant Permissions
1. When prompted, tap "Allow" for:
   - SMS permissions
   - Contact access
   - Phone state access

### Send Your First SMS
1. Import contacts (CSV or from phone)
2. Create a group (optional)
3. Select recipients
4. Type your message
5. Click "Send"
